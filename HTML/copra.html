Welcome to my Agri-Business Page: Information on Copra


Below is a brief information on Copra.



What is Copra? Copra is the dried meat or kernel of the coconut, which is the fruit of the coconut palm. Coconut oil is extracted from copra, making it an impotant agraicultural comodity for many coconut producing countries. It also yields de-fatted coconut cake after oil extraction which is Mainly used as feed for livestock. There ere many products produced from copra.

Some products of copra are:



  1. Coconut oil
  2. Coconut milk
  3. Coconut cream
  4. Coconut flakes
  5. Coconut butter
